Project Concept

Project Anano explores a world where nature evolved beyond human scale. Instead of destroying civilization, the environment absorbed it. Cities, roads, and infrastructure remain visible but are intertwined with massive ecosystems.

World Design

The world emphasizes the coexistence of old civilization and accelerated ecosystems. Massive trees grow through buildings, vines wrap around infrastructure, and nature integrates itself into human structures.

Core Gameplay Loop

Exploration, mutation discovery, and relic hunting form the core player loop. Players traverse the world, encounter mutated creatures, and unlock abilities that allow deeper exploration.

Mutation System

Creatures in the world possess unique mutations. After defeating them, players can extract mutation cores and equip abilities that modify traversal and combat mechanics.

Relic Hunting

Relics are fragments of the old world requested by settlements inside the dome. These objects are everyday artifacts such as books, cassette players, or technology devices.

Recovering relics expands the narrative and reveals the history of the collapsed civilization.
