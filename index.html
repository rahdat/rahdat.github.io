Selected Projects

Kartboard Dash Releasing Soon

Kartboard Dash is an arcade kart racing game currently in development at Zatun Games for PC and mobile with cross-platform play. As Lead Game Designer, I defined the core gameplay systems that shape driving mechanics, race dynamics, and player progression.

  • Designed the full vehicle handling model balancing responsive driving, drift mechanics, and accessibility across keyboard, controller, and touch inputs.
  • Created the powerup and perk modifier system introducing strategic race interactions while maintaining competitive balance.
  • Shaped track flow and race pacing, collaborating with environment artists to support readable racing lines and high-speed gameplay.
  • Designed the player progression framework including unlockable perks, upgrades, and reward loops supporting both short sessions and long-term engagement.

Project Pneuma Releasing Soon

Project Pneuma is a survival horror experience currently in development at Zatun Games, focused on psychological tension and environmental storytelling. My work centered on designing systems that control gameplay pressure, player vulnerability, and encounter pacing.

  • Designed a dynamic tension system that adapts encounter intensity based on player behavior to sustain psychological pressure.
  • Built the sanity system, linking player stability to environmental distortions, gameplay feedback, and enemy behavior changes.
  • Developed the stealth and encounter framework, encouraging observation, navigation, and resource management over direct combat.
  • Collaborated closely with art and engineering teams to align gameplay systems with narrative tone and environmental storytelling.
